My Sticky Bun Recipe!

It takes about a 1/2 hour to prepare and another 1/2 hour to bake and cool, but BOY is it ever worth the TIME!

Dough Ingredients:

  • 2-1/2 cups all purpose flour (I prefer a 50/50 mix of whole wheat flour and white flour.)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons butter (snipped)
  • 3/4 cup milk (it tastes best with whole milk, but any will do)

Filling Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons melted butter (Please, please, please don't use margerine unless you're allergic to butter!)
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • pinch of cardomom

Topping Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Blend Dough ingredients together starting with dry ingredients, stir in butter and milk until just mixed.
  3. Flour surface and knead the dough until pliable, but not sticky.
  4. Roll out onto floured surface with rolling pin.
  5. Mix all Filling ingredients together and spread onto rolled out dough.
  6. Starting at long end of dough, roll up, and slice the "log" roll into 12 rolls.
  7. Mix all Topping ingredients together. Set aside.
  8. Spread topping on the bottom of a 9" x 9" baking pan.
  9. Place rolls, cut side down, into the pan on top of the topping.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes. Buns will be golden on sides and top.
  11. Flip buns out of pan (UPSIDEDOWN) onto a piece of parchment paper. Cool and EAT UP!
