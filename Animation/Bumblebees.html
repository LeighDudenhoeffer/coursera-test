Bumblebees Polinate Everything!

We all know that honeybees are a very popular type of polinator, but the mighty bumblebee is one of the best polinators around. We wouldn't have all of the wonderful flowers and food we have without polination. There are many different insects that polinate for us, but usually honey bees are the go to for pollination.

The bumblebee is a free living creature that's a lot larger than most bees and wasps.
